Our Beginning

A Dream Built With Love

Hiyswa Jambu Homestay started with a simple vision — to create a space where families, friends, and loved ones can come together and truly unwind. We believe that the best memories are made in places that feel like home, and that is exactly what we have built for you.

Our homestay is designed with your comfort in mind. From the moment you arrive, you'll be welcomed by lush greenery, the sound of splashing water from our pool and mini water park, and the warm smiles of our team who are dedicated to making your stay unforgettable.

"We don't just rent rooms — we create experiences that families will talk about for years to come."

Whether you're here for a family holiday, a birthday celebration, a team outing, or simply a weekend escape, Hiyswa Jambu offers everything you need under one roof. Our six world-class facilities — including a swimming pool, mini water park, futsal court, spacious event space, BBQ area, and bicycle rentals — ensure there's something for everyone, young and old alike.

We are proudly a family-owned and operated homestay, and this shows in everything we do — from the carefully maintained facilities to the thoughtful touches in every room. At Hiyswa Jambu, you are not just a guest. You are part of our family.
